Bioko - Chapter II

Chapter II is work in progress!

Recently, I mapped lava flows on remotely sensed satellite imagery to measure their length. On a TSX/TDX DEM a couple of indicators such as channel / levee structures can be observed. During the fieldwork campaign in Nov / Dec 2022, I measured flow heights. Based on these parameters, I'm creating probabilistic hazard maps for Bioko using Q-LavHa (Mossoux et al. 2016).

Check ResearchGate for the first results that I presented at the IUGG 2023 in Berlin this summer!
