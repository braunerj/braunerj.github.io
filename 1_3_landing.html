Bioko

My PhD project consists of three chapters: (I) A structural inventory of volcanic features, (II) A lava flow hazard assessment, and (III) An analysis of landslides and other processes that are linked to the formation of erosional calderas. I utilize drone- and satellite-borne mapping (SAR, LiDAR, Photogrammetry, Optical) to model volcanic processes and associated hazards (QGIS / ArcGIS, ENVI, Python, analogue).

Chapter I

Shield volcano morphology and how it is linked to regional tectonics and local volcano-tectonic processes.
