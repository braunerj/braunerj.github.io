Bioko - Chapter II

Chapter II is work in progress!

Recently, I tried to identify lava flows on remotely sensed satellite imagery. The big problem is the dense cover of jungle canopy on the island. EO-1 Hyperion (insufficient coverage), Landsat-8, MODIS (MODVOLC) and Sentinel-1 images haven't delivered any results so far. On a TSX/TDX DEM a couple of indicators such as channel / levee structures can be observed. I will also try to track down recent lava flows by GEDI LiDAR data and hyperspectral PRISMA imagery. The next step will be to characterize and reconstruct the identified lava flows and to create a hazard map (DOWNFLOW / Q-lavha / FLOWGO).

Stay tuned for updates!
