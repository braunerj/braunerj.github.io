Research

I am a PhD student at the Drexel University and my doctoral studies are focusing on mapping, modeling, and assessment of volcanic structures, related processes and hazards on Bioko Island (Equatorial Guinea). Previous projects were based on classical fieldwork / mapping, facies analysis, and geochemistry.
